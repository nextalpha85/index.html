Game Updates

Crit damage has been adjusted to be more appropriate.

Crit damage: now 1% per attribute point (1 point = 1% of total base damage).

Usable Item Slots

Auto-continue has been disabled when "Return to Town" is clicked. Now, auto-continue will be forced to end.

Energy system has been updated:

  • Start with 10 energy
  • Max 50 energy
  • Gain +1 energy per level

Refill Max Energy option is now available in Well menu for 20 gold (once per day).

Combat bag filtered to show only usable and consumable items.

Skill cooldowns will now show in the battle menu for all skills.
